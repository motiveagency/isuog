  • Discounted registration fees for ISUOG World Congress and all other ISUOG events. See our website for all upcoming courses near you www.isuog.org/events
  • Access to our online learning CME credit platform, ISUOG Academy
  • Access to VISUOG: Our Visual Encyclopedia for Ultrasound in Obstetrics and Gynecology
  • Opportunities to participate in our ‘Outreach’ projects and volunteering programmes
